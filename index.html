About The ToyBoat Company

We began with a dream — to revive the charm of handcrafted toys in a world ruled by plastic and automation. Each toyboat we make is born from patient craftsmanship, ethical sourcing, and an undying respect for artistry. We don’t just build toyboats — we build legacies meant to be cherished across generations.
